Q. When will Senior Formal prices and tickets be released?

Tickets went live April 11th at 12:00pm. See the sections on Tickets & Transportation for more information and the link to purchase tickets. Tickets will include two drinks, hors d'oeuvres, and of course great music, great venue, great friends! We’ll also have lots of fun activities during the night, including a photobooth, two dance floors, and live performances.

Q. Can I bring a non-Senior or a non-Stanford student to Senior Formal?

Yes, tickets will be available for non-Seniors provided they are accompanied by a member of the Class of 2013 (these will be available at the standard price). Tickets will also be available for non-Stanford students, though there may be a surcharge.

Q. Will I need tickets for Bing Senior Arts Gala?

The event is definitely free. You might need to get a ticket in advance... we'll let you know over email.

Q. How can I get tickets for the San Francisco Senior Night?

Due to the extremely positive feedback from last quarter's SF Senior Night, we wanted to provide the class with another event in the city. We will be helping coordinate transportation for more people, however, due again to our limited funds and the high expenses associated with transportation to San Francisco, we will only be able to provide limited seats on buses for free. When these tickets become available, we will send out a link to the Senior Class as well as post it on a Facebook event similar to the process from last quarter.

Q. Do I need to buy tickets for Senior Workshops?

No tickets will be required for Senior Workshops! However, space will be limited at these events (we haven't quite set a cap on them yet), so we will communicate more details on whether you will have to sign up or whether they will be first-come-first-serve events!

Q. What time will the Senior Workshops be? Timing of the Senior Workshops is TBA, but plan on them being in the evening after dinner.

Q. I have more questions! Who should I contact?

You can email any member of the Class of 2013 Cabinet. Alternatively, you can email stanfordscp2013@gmail.com, and we will do our best to get back to you as soon as possible!

Tickets
Tickets to Senior Formal include two drinks (alcoholic or non-alcoholic), hors d'oeuvres, and of course great music, great venue, great friends!
There are several different types of tickets. All Student tickets require the ticket holders to present a valid Stanford SUID to be admitted into the event. ALL ticket holder must present valid government issued photo ID at the door to be admitted into the event.
You have the option of purchasing up to 4 additional drink tickets (alcoholic @ $7 or non-alcoholic @ $4) after selecting your ticket -- alternatively, drink tickets will be available at the venue through cash or credit payments.

Senior Formal Early Bird Student Ticket: $64 online only (+ applicable fees)
Senior Formal Student Ticket: $74 online (+ applicable fees) or in cash (no fees, availability TBA)
Senior Formal non-Stanford Guest Ticket: $84 online only (+ applicable fees)

Transportation
Round trip bus tickets will provide access to transportation to and from the event. Buses will leave Tresidder at 9:00pm and return from the venue at 2:00am.
Roundtrip Bus Ticket: $20 online only (incl. fees)

All event ticket sales end at 12:00pm on the day of the event, May 17th -- tickets for entry will be available at the door. Bus ticket sales will end one week prior to the event, May 10th, at 5:00pm.

If you are interested in becoming the one and only Commencement weekend student speaker this year, please read carefully!!
The Baccalaureate Student Speaker Contest offers the only chance for a student to speak to the class during Commencement weekend. It is a high honor and a chance to reflect on how your experiences at Stanford -- personal, moral, or spiritual -- have shaped who you are today and how they can inspire your classmates as they embark upon the next chapter of their lives.

  • Speech cannot exceed 600 words
  • Entries accepted Tues. April 30th - Fri. May 3rd @ 5:00PM. No exception to the deadline and please send your speech in Microsoft Word format only to baccalaureate@lists.stanford.edu.
  • Finalists will be contacted by May 7th at midnight and MUST be available for auditions on Friday, May 10th from 10:00AM to 12:00PM.
  • A $500 prize will be awarded to the winner.
